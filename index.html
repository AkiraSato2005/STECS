Mission

Our mission is to provide quality education that nurtures the holistic development of students, instilling in them values of integrity, excellence, and service to others.

Vision

Our vision is to be a leading educational institution that empowers students to become compassionate and competent leaders in a global society.

Brief History

Founded in 19XX, Saint Edriel Catholic School has been a pillar of academic excellence in the community, serving generations of learners with dedication and commitment.
